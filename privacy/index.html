2. What ConvOps Does

ConvOps connects your infrastructure monitoring (e.g. AWS CloudWatch) to WhatsApp. When an alert fires, ConvOps:

  • Receives the alert event from your monitoring system
  • Analyses the alert context using AI (Anthropic Claude)
  • Sends a WhatsApp notification to the authorised on-call engineer
  • Allows the engineer to reply via WhatsApp to trigger pre-approved remediation actions (e.g. scaling, restarts)
  • Logs the action taken for your audit trail

3. Data We Collect and Process

We process the minimum data necessary to operate the service. We store operational metadata only:

Data Type Description Retention
Alert events Alarm name, state (OK / ALARM), timestamp, metric name — as sent by your monitoring system 90 days
Conversation state Current conversation step and pending action for each active alert session Deleted on session close; max 24 h
Audit logs Record of actions taken: who approved, what action, outcome, timestamp 90 days
Customer account data Business name, contact email, WhatsApp number(s) for on-call routing Duration of contract + 30 days

We do NOT store:

  • Raw application logs or log content
  • Secrets, credentials, or API keys
  • Personally identifiable information beyond business contact details
  • Message content beyond what is needed to route and confirm the current action

4. Lawful Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process personal data under Article 6(1)(b) GDPR — processing is necessary for the performance of a contract to which the data subject is party (i.e. the service agreement between ConvOps and the customer, and the on-call engineer's use of the service as authorised by their employer).

Where applicable, we also rely on Article 6(1)(f) (legitimate interests) for fraud prevention and security logging.

5. How We Use Your Data

  • Routing infrastructure alerts to the correct on-call engineer via WhatsApp
  • Enabling authorised remediation actions through conversational replies
  • Generating AI-assisted summaries of alert context (Anthropic Claude)
  • Maintaining an audit trail of actions taken on your infrastructure
  • Diagnosing service issues and ensuring reliability

We do not sell, share, or use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to operating ConvOps.

Sub-Processor Purpose Data Location

7. International Data Transfers

Our primary infrastructure is located in the EU (AWS eu-central-1, Frankfurt). Alert context data is transmitted to Anthropic (US) for AI analysis, and WhatsApp message delivery is handled by Meta's global infrastructure.

Transfers to the US are made under Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) as provided by each sub-processor's data processing agreement.
