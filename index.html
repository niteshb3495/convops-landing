Built for engineers who don't trust black boxes

ConvOps has read access to your AWS account and optional write access for approved actions. Here's exactly what that means:

🔒 Read-only by default
ConvOps reads metrics, logs, and resource state. No write access unless you explicitly enable it during setup.
🙋 No automatic actions — ever
Nothing executes without your explicit reply. "YES" confirmation required. Phone-bound — only you can approve your alerts.
🎯 Scoped IAM permissions
One CloudFormation template. ConvOps gets exactly the permissions it needs — nothing more. You can review or delete the IAM role anytime.
📋 Full audit logs
Every alert, investigation, and action is logged with timestamp, resource, and outcome. 90-day retention.
⏱ 15-min session limit
STS tokens expire after 15 minutes. No long-lived credentials. No credentials stored outside your AWS account.
🗑 Revoke instantly
Delete the IAM role in your AWS account. ConvOps loses all access immediately. No support ticket needed.
