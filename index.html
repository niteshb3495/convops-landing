Your infrastructure. Your control.

ConvOps has access to your AWS account. Here's exactly what that means — no vague claims.

Read-only by default

ConvOps reads CloudWatch metrics, logs, and resource state. No write access until you explicitly enable it. You choose which services can be acted on.

Manual approval required

No action executes automatically. Ever. You reply YES to confirm, NO to cancel. Confirmation is phone-bound — only you can approve your alerts. Expires after 5 minutes.

Full audit logs

Every investigation and action logged with timestamp, resource ARN, who approved it, and outcome. 90-day retention. You can export anytime.

🎯
Scoped IAM permissions

One CloudFormation template. Minimum permissions needed — nothing more. Review or delete the IAM role anytime from your AWS console.

⏱
15-min STS sessions

Tokens expire after 15 minutes. No long-lived credentials stored outside your AWS account.

🗑
Revoke instantly

Delete the IAM role from your AWS account. ConvOps loses all access immediately — no support ticket needed.
