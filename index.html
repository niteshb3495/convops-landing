Security & Control

ConvOps is an AI agent with cloud access. Here's how we keep it safe:

✅ Read-only by default - You explicitly grant write permissions per resource

✅ Approval workflow - ALL destructive actions require human confirmation

✅ Full audit log - Every command, approval, and execution logged with timestamps

✅ AWS IAM roles - No long-lived credentials stored. Session tokens only.

✅ Encrypted everything - TLS in transit, AES-256 at rest

✅ Self-hosted option - Runs in YOUR AWS account (your cloud, your data)

What We DON'T Do:

❌ No autonomous actions without approval

❌ No training on your data

❌ No credential storage (IAM roles only)

❌ No third-party data sharing
